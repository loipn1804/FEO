The Beginning...

Three Brothers founded Far East Flora Holdings since 1965. With a van, they peddled door-to-door, selling delicate flowers and eggs, and earning daily sales of $100. Though the returns were enough to sustain the family business, the brothers were on to bigger and bolder dreams.

It was a few years later that the brothers decided to embark on the wholesale business of exporting orchids, which proved to be a success. And it was from here that the business expanded both locally and globally. Far East Orchid Pte Ltd, Fresh Cut Flowers Wholesale Department was established in 1976.

Today...

Far East Flora Holdings operates wholesale floral centre(www.FarEastOrchid.com) along with other business units ranging from garden centre (www.FarEastFloraGarden.com), online florist (www.FarEastFlora.com), landscaping, interior plantscaping & plant rental (www.o2plants.com.sg) and has also expanded internationally to Hong Kong and Malaysia.

Far East Orchid Pte Ltd launched its online wholesale floral centre in 2013.
