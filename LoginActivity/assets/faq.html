How to keep cut flowers fresh?

 

Keep flowers out of direct sunlight.

Remove leaves that will be under water in the arrangement.

Add Chrysal Flower Food in the water.

Cut stems at a slant with a sharp knife/scissors and plunged immediately into water to prevent air bubbles.

Display at room temperature and avoid direct sunlight, warm or humid environment.

Change the water every day.

Repeat until flowers are discarded.
